About Lark
Lark is the world's largest A.I. healthcare provider, servicing nearly 2 million patients living with or at risk of a chronic disease. Lark is a companion app on the user’s phone providing real-time coaching, guidance, and health insights to users. We are on a mission to disrupt the healthcare industry and make the world a healthier, happier place. Come join our team!

The Role
As Lark’s Marketing Analyst, you will be the analytical arm of our marketing activities. You will provide the data driven view-point to our marketing campaigns, own the measurement and data accuracy of our marketing data, and develop strategic analysis to improve our user enrollment, engagement and retention metrics.

What You’ll Do
You will develop new approaches to grow our user enrollments
You will build visualizations to evaluate the performance of our different marketing channels and make strategic decisions
You will generate data driven insights for our marketing campaigns
You will oversee and run different tests and experiments to efficiently scale our user base

What You’ll Need
Experience with SQL or Python
Excel/Google sheets expertise
Confidence using a variety of techniques to connect and analyze large data sets
Experience with marketing analytics tools such as Google analytics, Google tag manager, and Optimizely
Experience building visualizations with visualization tools such as Tableau, Periscope, Looker or equivalent
Experience with marketing measurement methodologies (marketing mix models and multi-touch attribution)
Ability to articulate complex concepts to a non-technical audience
Strong written and verbal communication skills required
Previous experience in a growing startup is a plus
