The Product Manager / Business Analyst role will collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to refine the product vision, product roadmap, build product requirements documents with detailed functional requirements, and become a subject matter expert for the products, all from within the Web Development department.

The ideal candidate is someone who has worked in ecommerce or other consumer facing websites, as a product manager, business analyst or other technical or marketing role. The candidate should have an aptitude and interest for defining product requirements and direction and can work with a wide variety of internal and external stakeholders.

Duties
Manage day-to-day operations, clarify requirements, analyze business value and prioritize development efforts for multiple technology projects of various complexities.
Work in conjunction with a team of Web Developers, Test Case Writers, Quality Assurance Analysts, Project Managers, Copywriters, UX Designers, and Business Units to develop new products and to provide ongoing management for existing products
Subject matter expert and technology evangelist for the web properties, apps, and APIs, including providing transfer of information and product familiarization on both current product functionality and new product enhancements throughout the company, and to third-party integrators and integration projects
Own product requirement definition and documentation
Develop requirements documents that define product enhancements and development initiatives
Conduct product brainstorm sessions with inter-departmental teams to define product and technology requirements and strategies
Participate in product design activities, wireframes, flow charting, and mockups that generate user friendly solutions that provide clear end-user benefits and differentiation from competitor products
Assess stakeholder needs, technology trends, and major competitors in order to synthesize product and technical requirements
